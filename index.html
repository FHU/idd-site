What is
Interactive
Digital
Design

Beauty + Function

Interactive Digital Design is an interdisciplinary major that combines graphic design and software development.

Students develop skills in creating software products that are functional and beautiful.

Choose your Focus

The IDD curriculum begins with 8 core classes, 4 computer science classes and 4 art classes. Then students may choose one of two tracks within the IDD major: Developer or Designer.

Developer

The developer track (B.S.) prepares students who want to specialize in technical aspects of application development. It includes courses in web design, databases, and mobile application development.

Designer

The designer track (B.A.) emphasizes graphic design and digital photography in its advanced coursework. Both tracks equip students with programming and visual design experience but allow the student to specialize their degree to suit their strengths.

What Will I
Create

Real websites, applications, products, posters and more created for real people, by real Freed-Hardman University students. Our students are given the opportunity to not only learn the technique and skills required for interactive digital media, but are also able to work on real-life projects and gain experience in carrying out projects from beginning to end. What kind of
Jobs
Can I Get

A degree in Interactive Digital Design prepares you for a range of design and development jobs for both the web and mobile app platforms.

Students with greater technical inclination will be prepared to get jobs as web developers, mobile app developers, and software developers. Graduates with greater interest in design will be prepared to apply those skills to web and app design in addition to positions like user experience designer and user interface designer.
