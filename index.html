What Kind of Jobs Can I Get

A degree in Interactive Digital Design prepares you for a range of design and development jobs for both the web and mobile app platform. Students with greater technical inclination will be prepared to get jobs as web developers, mobile app developers, and software developers. Graduates with greater interest in design will be prepared to apply those skills to web and app design in addition to positions like user experience designer and user interface designer.
